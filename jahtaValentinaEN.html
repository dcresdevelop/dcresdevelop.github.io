Apartment description

The apartment is located on the ground floor of a private house. The ground floor of the apartment is adapted for wheelchairs, it can be placed at the entrance the ramp and the corridor have a built-in ramp. The bathroom on the ground floor is also adapted for people with disabilities. The apartment has two separate rooms on the ground floor and one room for 3 people on the first floor. Lighting and air conditioning in the apartment is automatic.

It is located right next to the promenade itself and is across along the sea promenade. The apartment is designed in the shape of a yacht (ship floor, ship windows, details...).
