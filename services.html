Make your vacation unforgettable

Collaborative travel agency Cres Fun & More

The tourist agency Cres Fun & More specializes in small group tours. Our excursions are designed as a complete experience of local life through local food, attractions, local traditions, relaxing in the most beautiful places and meeting local people.
Fun tours in small groups are a great way to meet people and create wonderful a memory of our beautiful island. The tours are led by local guides who actually live here.
Guests can contact them for any tourist information and practical advice which can be used for travel planning, so that the vacation can be completely relaxed.

You can also contact us for the organization of excursions and vacations, customized arrangements, various corporate activities or if you need to hire a tourist or hiking guide for yourself. The Cres Fun & More team is at your disposal on a professional and creative level.
